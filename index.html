HAPI Hack Weekend is a hackathon focused on leveraging APIs to create cool new software. The event brings together designers and developers for a chance to win prizes, create products and meet talented people.

Event Overview

The event starts Friday, June 10 when attendees will hear lightning talks from leading companies on how to use their API. Following the talks, attendees can then pitch ideas and form teams for building their apps.

Attendees will spend the rest of the weekend hacking on their ideas and will also have the opportunity to attend workshops on getting started with the various APIs during the day on Saturday. The event will conclude Sunday afternoon with demo presentations, judging, and awards.
